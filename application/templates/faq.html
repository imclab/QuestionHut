FAQ
What kind of questions should I ask here?
On this site, you can ask questions about your class, homework, or logistics. You can ask questions about material you were confused about in lecture, or clarifications on the assignment. Most of the questions you have someone else will have as well. Asking questions will benefit the rest of the class.
What kind of questions should I not ask here?
You should not give any information away about specific parts of your code. Code is generally not allowed, except for asking questions about syntax. Questions will be moderated to make sure they are appropriate to be posted. You shouldn't ask questions where you just ask someone to give you the answer. You should show that you have put in effort before asking.
Is this a Stack Overflow clone?
Yes, this is a Stack Overflow clone. They do lots of things right in terms of building a question and answer website. If you've been on Stack Overflow, you know the one type of questions they really don't like is homework questions. But that's what this is for. It's a question answer site for your classes.
What is reputation?
Reputation is the number next to your name, and it is how much you have contributed to the community. The way to get reputation is by writing questions and answers that other people like.
answer is voted up +10
question is voted up +5
answer is accepted +15 (+2 to acceptor)
question is voted down -2
answer is voted down -2 (-1 to voter)
